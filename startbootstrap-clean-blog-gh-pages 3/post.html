Welcome to My Professional Journey

The path of a dedicated marketing professional

Currently serving as a marketing professional, I am fully engaged in creating impactful marketing strategies and campaigns. My role involves not just conceptualizing ideas but also ensuring they translate into tangible results. This experience has been integral in honing my skills in marketing analytics and business development.

As I near the completion of my MBA at Brigham Young University's Marriott School of Business this April, I am eager to begin the next phase of my career at Verizon. This transition is a testament to my commitment to continuous learning and growth. Verizon offers a platform where I can utilize my skills to contribute to innovative solutions and cutting-edge marketing strategies.

My ambition is to evolve into a leadership role, specifically as a Product Management Officer, where I can influence the product lifecycle and strategy. I am also deeply committed to mentorship, particularly advocating for women in technology. My desire is to inspire and guide women to pursue and excel in technology careers. In addition, I plan to give back to the Marriott School of Business by mentoring students and helping them carve their own success stories.
